Intro

A community where people can work together towards the common goal. A place to connect and share learning, knowledge or skills and receive consistent support from a built-in network.

We want to foster a feeling of community spirit and identity. To promote participation by providing the opportunity for everyone to share the Tweebaa experience, and benefit from it at a greater level; building relationships with likeminded people, who share their interests, culture and identity. Tweebaa challenges the whole concept of online commerce. It costs nothing to sign up and has its very own EcoSystem, in which trading is done with its virtual currency; the Tweebuck. Tweebucks work like any other currency, but they are only available to use within Tweebaa.

We also have our own rewards system; TiV (Tweebaa Influencer Value). It is a unique measurement system developed by Tweebaa to reward you for each and every activity you perform within the Tweebaa platform, to ensure that even your social media activity has value and can generate earnings.

You start gaining TiV points and earning Tweebucks as soon as you sign up to Tweebaa and you can decide to convert them to cash at any time by selling back into the Market. What makes this so unique is that you also have the opportunity to invest your Tweebucks by converting them into Tweebaa’s own Cryptocurrency, the TWEE, therein reinvesting into the EcoSystem for greater gains and becoming a bigger part of Tweebaa’s future.

You have the control over your earnings, whether you want a regular income, to save your earnings or to even become an investor in the company. The potential is unlimited!

一旦您注册了推易吧，您就可以开始获得TiV积分并赚取T豆，您可以决定随时随地将T豆卖回市场变现。推易巴的独特之处在于，您还可以将T豆转换为推易吧自己的加密货币TWEE, 这其实是再次投资到推易吧的生态中，以获得更大的收益，并成为推易吧未来的一分子。

When those product suggestions are received by Tweebaa, they are entered into an Evaluation stage. You are able to earn simply by evaluating these products and have the opportunity to provide your views on which products you think would be popular. Evaluation takes just a couple of minutes and involves answering a few yes/no questions and then adding a comment about the product. It is that simple and the more you evaluate, the more you earn! Did you know that you can earn simply by suggesting products that you think will sell on Tweebaa? For example, if you use a great product that you love and think other people would like to buy it, you can suggest it to Tweebaa. You can suggest both physical and digital products. When you suggest a product, Tweebaa will evaluate it and if approved, they will then locate the product and list it for Presale. Once there is enough interest shown during Presale, the product will be added to the Product Showroom and you will start earning Tycoon Profit (commission) on every sale made, anywhere in the world, for as long as the product is sold on Tweebaa!
