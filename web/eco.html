Product Progress
价值变现

Discover the products of tomorrow or watch your products rise to the top on Tweebaa. Learn a bit more about how you can achieve that with our step-by-step walkthrough.

在推易吧上发现新产品或让你的产品上升到推易吧的顶部。通过以下的步骤，你将更加了解如何实现你的目标。

1
Suggest
建议产品

The first stage is to suggest a product that you feel is unique and would sell. As a Tycoon, you are able to suggest any type of product: Physical, Digital, Self-Made, Existing For every suggestion you make that advances to the Product Showroom, you can earn a lifetime commission off of every sale that product makes!

作为一个推易吧的“推主”，你可以推荐以下四种不同类型的产品: 实体产品，数字产品，自创产品，现有产品 任何经由你推荐，并经过评审进入推易吧产品库进行销售的产品都将支付你终身佣金以示感谢

2
Evaluate
评估产品

The second stage is to evaluate products on the Discover Page in the Tweebaa app. To evaluate, just simply 1) Answer a few yes-no questions
2) Add a comment on the product If your product receives enough positive evaluations, it will advance to the Presale stage. The additional benefits are that, by completing evaluations, you will receive a TiV reward and earn a commission reward if the product reaches the Product Showroom.

在推易吧的“发现“页面上可以评估其他推主建议的产品： 如果某一产品得到足够多的正面评价，它将进入预售阶段。 通过完成评估，你将获得TiV奖励；并且当该产品进入推易吧产品库进行销售时你将获得评估佣金。

3
Presale
预售

The third stage is presale. This stage tests how well your product will perform in our market.
A. If you reach your presale goal, your product will then be featured in the Product Showroom.
B. If you are the inventor of a product, you can also submit your product to the presale room. If your product has been approved by Tweebaa, it will be displayed on the presale stage.

预售阶段是测试你的产品在推易吧市场上的表现如何。
一. 如果你的产品达到了预售目标，你的产品将会进入推易吧产品库。
二. 如果你是一个产品发明者，你也可以提交你的产品到推易吧预售。经过推易吧的审核，你的产品就会在预售阶段进行展示。

4
Showroom
推易吧产品库

The fourth stage is the Product Showroom. Here your product is given a global platform to Tycoons worldwide and can now be purchased and sold by other Tycoons.

当你的产品进入推易吧产品库。世界各地的推主都可以进行购买，或者把你的产品添加到他们的个人空间进行转售。。

TiV
TiV（影响力值）
Your TiV, or your Tweebaa influence Value, is a measurement of your level of engagement on Tweebaa. The more engaged you are, the higher your score and the more rewards you will receive. By conducting various activities like sharing products, suggesting your ideas, generating sales, interacting with other Tycoons, and more, you can increase your TiV score.
Your TiV score is used to determine the sales commission from your products or Tweebaa production sales and your monthly extra cash reward.
你的TiV，或者你的推易吧影响力值，是衡量你在推易吧上参与度的指标。你的参与度越高，你的分数越高，你得到的奖励也就越多。通过进行各种活动，如分享，点赞，推荐产品，评审产品，销售，与其他推主互动等等，可以提高你的TiV值。 TiV值是用来决定你购买产品时的折扣额度，或销售产品时获得佣金额度的重要依据。同时也是推易吧每月对你进行额外奖励的依据。
你的TiV分数是用来决定你的产品或推易吧生产销售的销售佣金和你每月额外的现金奖励主要依据。
Tweebucks
T豆
On Tweebaa, whether you’re making a purchase or getting paid, our Tycoons use Tweebucks. Tweebucks work like any other currency, but they are only available to purchase and use in our Tycoon-place.
While you can purchase Tweebucks (1 Tweebuck = $0.10USD), you can also get them for FREE from earning TiV! Your TiV is automatically converted into Tweebucks, so the more TiV you earn from your engagement, the more Tweebucks you get.
在推易吧，无论你是购物还是赚钱，都是使用T豆进行交易。T都是推易吧内唯一流通的货币。你可以以1 推币= 0.10美元的价格进行购买
也可以通过赚取TiV值来免费获得到T豆，TiV值会自动转换成T豆。 所以你在推易吧的参与越多，从活动中获得的TiV值就越多。
